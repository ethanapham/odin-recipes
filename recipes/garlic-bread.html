Cheesy Garlic Bread

Home

Description

A fresh cheesy garlic bread can be easily made with fresh ingredients found in your local bakery and Safeway!

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of Italian bread
  • Olive oil
  • 2 sticks of butter
  • 1 head of garlic (will be roasted)
  • 1 clove of garlic (fresh)
  • Salt and pepper
  • 3 tbsp freshly chopped parsley
  • 2 cups (or however much you want) grated mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cut a whole head of garlic in half and lay them face up (bulbs should be exposed) on a long piece of tin foil.
  2. Drizzle across both heads with olive oil and then hit them with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. (OPTIONAL) Throw in some fresh thyme next to the garlic.
  4. Wrap the contents up in the tin foil into a ball and put into a 300 degree oven for about 45 minutes - 1 hour.
  5. In the meantime, get a bowl and throw your two sticks of butter in there. You want it a little warm/melted, so leave it out while the garlic cooks.
  6. Once the garlic cools off, take it out of the oven, and squeeze the roasted bulbs into the softened butter.
  7. Mince your fresh garlic and throw it in as well. Also, add a little bit of freshly chopped parsley + salt and pepper.
  8. Mix with your hands or a spoon until you get a nicely mixed compound butter.
  9. Cut open in half your loaf of Italian bread.
  10. Spread a nice even layer of your compound butter on each loaf, and put them into a 400 degree oven (Around 5-10 minutes)
  11. Take out the bread and cover the entire surface with the grated mozzarella (Don't leave any bread showing!)
  12. Put it back into the oven at 400 degrees for about 5-10 minutes again (or into the broiler).
  13. Enjoy!
